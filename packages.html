5 Star, Luxury Couples Resort & Spa

Couples Resort offers an all-inclusive, 5-star getaway for couples seeking romance and relaxation beside Algonquin Park. Each stay includes gourmet 5-course dining, breakfast in bed, a private lakeside hot tub, and a real wood-burning fireplace—plus over 25 complimentary bonuses with no tipping required. Guests enjoy seasonal activities, spa services, and exclusive free-night, honeymoon, and wedding packages. Eco-friendly and family-owned since 1967, the resort blends luxury, nature, and sustainability for an unforgettable romantic escape.

Last Minute Deals

Experience 5-Star Luxury and All-Inclusive Bliss – With Free Nights!

Take advantage of our limited packages offering unbeatable value: enjoy a 2-night escape and get the 3rd night free, or book a 5- or 7-night vacation and receive 2 nights free! Free nights are reflected directly in online pricing. For the best rates, try adjusting your stay by adding a night before and after your desired dates—you may unlock a significantly reduced rate or a bonus free night. Just repeat the search to compare!

Our all-inclusive packages feature luxuriously gorgeous accommodations, each with real fireplaces and private lakeside hot tubs. Enjoy 25 premium bonuses, including a 5-course gastronomic room-service dinner and breakfast in bed, prepared daily by our talented team of 8 chefs.

Whether you're joining us in winter, with guaranteed daily Ice Hiking and the chance to hand-feed Canada Jays and Chickadees, or in spring, when moose sightings are common and free motorboat rentals are available, adventure awaits! Activities include snowshoeing, cross-country skiing, ice skating, pickleball, tennis, and more. Spa services and daily guided hikes round out the experience.

Travel Tip: From May long weekend through October, beat the traffic when arriving from Toronto, Hamilton, or London by taking the 407 & 404 highways straight through Beaverton, Ontario—saving up to 2 hours of travel time!

Looking for something even more remote? Check out our sister property, Adventure Lodge, where couples can unwind in rustic log cabins for two with wood-burning fireplaces, some with whirlpools. All-inclusive stays include casual comfort dinners, breakfast, free park passes, and free use of canoes and kayaks.
