Room Styles

Chateaus

This 1,400-square-foot suite features a private outdoor hot tub, a private sauna, and a luxurious double shower equipped with a steam generator, four shower heads, and built-in benches, as well as a bidet for added comfort. Guests will enjoy a complimentary in-room basket stocked with lotions, bath salts, specialty products, and Soapstone (Bath and Body) items. The room also includes an Illy Espresso/Americano machine with capsules and a microwave for added convenience.

Villas

This 1,000-square-foot suite offers a private outdoor hot tub, a private sauna, and a double shower featuring a steam generator, four shower heads, and built-in benches, along with a bidet for added luxury. The room also includes an Illy Espresso/Americano machine with complimentary capsules, as well as a microwave for your convenience.

Masters

This 1,000-square-foot suite features a private outdoor hot tub and a double shower equipped with a steam generator, four shower heads, and built-in benches, as well as a bidet for added comfort. The room also includes a microwave for your convenience.
